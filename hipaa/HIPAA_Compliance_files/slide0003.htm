Who is HIPAA intended for?
nHIPAA aimed at health care providers and the people who do business with them:
nHealthcare providers – hospitals, pharmacies, nursing homes, home health agencies.
nHealth Plans – HMO’s MCO’s
nClearing houses – Billing services, re-pricing agencies third party administrators, life insurers, health Web sites
nBusiness associates – contractors, lawyers, data processing, etc. providing services to the above.
