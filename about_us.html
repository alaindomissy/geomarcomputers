TCO - Total Cost of Ownership

Whether you purchase a computer at a discount store, through a catalog or online, that purchase is just the beginning of the costs associated with owning that equipment. Over the life of that computer, you will have installation issues, questions, service and repairs, and the time that the computer cannot perform what you want it to do will be lost. These things and more go into what's called TCO - Total Cost of Ownership.

Getting a computer at a bargain basement sale may be an expensive decision in the long run. Smart business people and individuals recognize this, and anticipate the issues that will come up while owning the equipment.

Geomar Computers services what we sell, and can install and configure it for you on site. Choose a technology resource provider wisely to save yourself money - and headaches in the long run!
