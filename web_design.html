What does it cost to get a good business Web site?

A business can get an employee to try to do a web site for them and spend less than $1000, or a business might spend millions of dollars, so what's the difference? How does one justify, or estimate a proper, practical cost for a business Web site? Web sites are often estimated on a per page or per feature basis. Watch out for Web designers who want to bill you for time and materials - this is an open ended arrangement that can get you into budget trouble. If the company providing the quote does not know how much something costs to build they don't know the Web site business very well. Be sure to get an itemized, fixed written quote.
